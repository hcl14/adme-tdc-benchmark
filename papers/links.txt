SYNTHETIC ACCESSIBILITY / DRUG-LIKENESS FILTERS

WelQrate — gold standard small molecule benchmarking with PAINS filtering (2024)
  Paper: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC11601797/
